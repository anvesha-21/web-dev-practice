Motichoor Ladooo

🍊 Motichoor Laddu – Golden Pearls of Celebration Delicate, soft, and bursting with flavor, our Motichoor Laddu is a timeless treasure of Indian tradition. Made from tiny, fragrant pearls of besan (gram flour) fried to perfection in pure desi ghee and soaked in light saffron-kissed sugar syrup, each laddu is handcrafted with love. What sets our Motichoor Laddu apart is its melt-in-mouth texture, vibrant golden-orange hue, and subtle aroma of cardamom and rose. Whether it’s a wedding, festival, or sweet craving at home, this classic laddoo brings joy in every bite — just like Grandma used to make. 💛 Soft. Rich. Soulful. Because every celebration deserves a little golden magic.

Ghevar

🍯 Ghevar – The Royal Dessert of Rajasthan Crisp, golden, and soaked in sweetness — Ghevar is not just a dessert, it's an experience rooted in the royal kitchens of Rajasthan. Crafted with a delicate batter of flour, ghee, and chilled water, and deep-fried in a special mold to form its signature honeycomb texture, Ghevar is a true art of patience and perfection. Once fried to a golden crisp, it’s generously soaked in fragrant sugar syrup and topped with silver varq, slivered dry fruits, and a dash of cardamom. Often adorned with creamy rabri or saffron strands, Ghevar is the crown jewel of Teej and Raksha Bandhan celebrations. 🌼 Crispy. Sweet. Regal. One bite of our handcrafted Ghevar, and you’ll taste the heritage of India in its most festive form.

Pakeezah

🌸 Pakeezah Mithai – The Essence of Purity and Grace Elegant, soulful, and exquisitely handcrafted — Pakeezah Mithai is a tribute to timeless beauty and refined taste. Inspired by the word “Pakeezah”, meaning pure and divine, this luxurious sweet is made with the finest ingredients — delicate saffron, handpicked almonds, creamy khoya, and a whisper of rose essence. Soft in texture, mildly sweet, and beautifully adorned with silver varq and dried petals, Pakeezah isn’t just a mithai — it’s an expression of elegance and emotion. Perfect for those who appreciate subtle richness and artisanal charm. 🌿 Pakeezah Mithai – Where purity meets poetry in every bite.

Mewa Batti

🟠 Mewa Batti – A Royal Delight of Richness and Tradition Indulgent, festive, and full of flavor — Mewa Batti is a royal Rajasthani sweet that celebrates the richness of dry fruits and the warmth of tradition. Made with a rich mixture of mawa (khoya), cardamom, and saffron, each batti is hand-shaped and stuffed generously with chopped almonds, cashews, pistachios, and raisins. Slow-cooked and golden-baked to perfection, this luxurious sweet is often garnished with silver varq and rose petals, making it a festive centerpiece for weddings, pujas, and grand celebrations. 🌰 Mewa Batti is not just a sweet — it’s a bite of heritage, handcrafted for those who savor richness with every celebration.
